Self-Care Tips

5-minute meditation technique that changed my life

I wanted to share a simple breathing technique that has really helped me manage anxiety during exam periods. It's called the 4-7-8 method: breathe in for 4 counts, hold for 7, exhale for 8. Just 5 minutes of this before studying or sleeping has made such a difference. Hope this helps someone else too!
