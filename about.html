About MindCare AI

Our mission is to make student mental health support accessible, private, and actionable.

Our Story

MindCare AI began as a student mental health initiative focused on bridging gaps in access. Combining research-backed therapy techniques with privacy-first AI, we help students get timely support and connect with professionals when needed.

Our Values

  • Privacy & Consent
  • Evidence-based support
  • Accessibility and inclusion

Team & Partners

We collaborate with clinical psychologists, university counseling centers, and privacy experts to ensure the platform is both safe and effective.
